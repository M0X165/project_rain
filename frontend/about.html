Understanding how R.A.I.N. works

How It Works

1

Detection & Collection

Pressure and moisture sensors detect rain, hail, or snow. The opening shaft opens to collect all water.

2

Energy Conversion

Kinetic energy from falling drops converts to electricity and stores in capacitor fields.

3

Release Mechanism

When enough water is collected, the container tilts and opens the bottom shaft to empty.

4

Secondary Power

Falling water spins a water wheel, converting kinetic energy to electricity a second time.

💧 What happens to the water?

After the container gets emptied, the water is forwarded to water cleaning stations where it will be purified and bottled for future use.
